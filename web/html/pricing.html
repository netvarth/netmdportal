Pricing

Choose either the Full System or Monthly Subscription license depending on your needs and convenience.Both licensing system has 5 packages.Select a package depending upon your business volume. All packages will have the facilities of online result publishing and inter branch result transfer. Simple basic package is an ideal low-cost and low-risk solution for small labs and specimen collection centers. Premium,Premium+ and Enterprise package will have machine interfacing as well.
